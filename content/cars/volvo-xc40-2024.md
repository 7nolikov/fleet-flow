The Volvo XC40 Recharge brings Scandinavian design philosophy to electric motoring. Clean lines, sustainable materials, and the world's safest engineering—this is the thoughtful choice for the modern driver.

## Google Built-In

The XC40 Recharge features Google's Android Automotive OS, bringing Google Maps, Google Assistant, and the Play Store directly into the car. No phone connection needed.

## Electric Performance

With 408hp from dual electric motors and instant torque, the XC40 Recharge sprints to 100km/h in 4.9 seconds. Yet it's also remarkably efficient, with up to 418km of real-world range.
