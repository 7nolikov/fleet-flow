The Volkswagen Polo brings the legendary VW build quality to the supermini segment. Every detail, from the solid thunk of the doors to the precise steering, tells you this is a car built to last.

## Premium Feel, Practical Price

Inside, you'll find an interior that punches well above its weight class. Quality materials, intuitive controls, and a driving position that feels just right make every journey a pleasure. The 6-speed manual transmission is a joy to use, with a precise, satisfying shift action.

- **First-time renters**: Forgiving to drive with excellent visibility
- **Couples**: Comfortable for two with plenty of luggage space
- **Business travelers**: Professional appearance and reliable performance
- **Road trips**: Surprisingly capable on longer journeys with good fuel economy
