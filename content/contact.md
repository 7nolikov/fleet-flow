## Frequently Asked Questions

**What documents do I need?**  
A valid driving license (EU or international), passport or ID card, and proof of address.

**Is there a minimum rental period?**  
We have a 1-day minimum. Discounts available for rentals of 7+ days.

**What's included in the price?**  
Comprehensive insurance, unlimited kilometers, 24/7 roadside assistance, and all taxes.

**Can I cross borders?**  
Yes, travel within the EU is permitted. Please inform us in advance.
