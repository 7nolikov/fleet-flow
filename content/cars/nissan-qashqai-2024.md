The Nissan Qashqai invented the compact crossover segment, and it continues to set the standard. This latest generation brings sophisticated design, advanced technology, and the practical versatility that made the original such a hit.

## ProPILOT Technology

Nissan's ProPILOT system combines adaptive cruise control with lane centering and traffic jam assistance, reducing fatigue on long motorway journeys. It's semi-autonomous driving that actually works.

## Comfort Meets Capability

The Qashqai rides with the comfort of a sedan while offering the elevated seating position and practicality of an SUV. Its mild hybrid system adds efficiency without complexity.

## Perfect For

- **Mixed driving**: Equally at home in city and country
- **Technology fans**: Advanced driver assistance as standard
- **Small families**: Practical without being oversized
- **New SUV drivers**: Easy to drive and park despite its presence
