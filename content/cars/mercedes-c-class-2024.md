The new C-Class brings technologies from the flagship S-Class to the executive segment. The result is a car that pampers its occupants while delivering the refined driving experience Mercedes-Benz is famous for.

## S-Class Technology

The portrait-format central screen, borrowed directly from the S-Class, dominates the cabin with its crisp OLED display. The MBUX system learns your preferences and anticipates your needs.

## Plug-In Hybrid Excellence

With up to 100km of electric-only range, the C 300 e can cover most daily journeys without using a drop of fuel. When you do engage the petrol engine, the combined 313hp ensures effortless performance.
