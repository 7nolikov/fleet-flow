The Skoda Octavia is the smart choice for those who want more of everything. More interior space than cars costing twice as much. More standard equipment. More practicality. All wrapped in a design that's understated yet sophisticated.

## Legendary Space

With 600 litres of boot space (expandable to 1,555 litres), the Octavia swallows luggage like cars a class above. The rear seats offer limousine-like legroom, making this the perfect car for long journeys with passengers.

## Diesel Efficiency

The 2.0 TDI engine is ideal for covering long distances. Expect excellent fuel economy on motorways combined with the torque you need for effortless overtaking and mountain climbing.

## Perfect For

- **Long-distance travel**: Built for the autobahn and beyond
- **Families**: Room for everyone and everything
- **Business trips**: Executive space at a reasonable price
- **Golf trips**: Yes, it'll fit the clubs (and everyone else's too)
