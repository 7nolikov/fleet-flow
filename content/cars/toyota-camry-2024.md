The Toyota Camry has earned its reputation as one of the most reliable cars ever made. This hybrid version adds impressive fuel economy to that legendary dependability.

## Hybrid Efficiency

The 2.5-litre hybrid powertrain delivers exceptional fuel economy—expect around 4.5L/100km in mixed driving—while still providing ample performance for confident overtaking and motorway cruising.

## Toyota Reliability

There's a reason Toyota consistently tops reliability surveys. The Camry is engineered to go the distance, with every component designed for longevity and dependability.

- **Long-distance travelers**: Exceptional fuel economy for endless range
- **Reliability seekers**: Toyota's legendary dependability
- **Families**: Spacious, safe, and sensible
- **Business professionals**: Comfortable and economical
