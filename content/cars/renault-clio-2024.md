Meet the Renault Clio, a supermini that proves small cars can have big personalities. With its distinctive C-shaped LED lights and flowing lines, the Clio turns heads wherever it goes while delivering exceptional fuel economy.

## The Perfect City Companion

The Clio excels in urban environments where its compact dimensions make parking a breeze and its responsive 1.0-litre turbo engine provides plenty of zip for navigating traffic. Yet it's equally comfortable on longer journeys, with a refined ride and surprisingly spacious interior that belies its compact footprint.
