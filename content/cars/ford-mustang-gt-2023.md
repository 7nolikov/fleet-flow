## Pure American Muscle

450 horsepower from a naturally aspirated V8, sent to the rear wheels through a 6-speed manual transmission. The Mustang GT is old-school performance in the best possible way.

The Mustang's design has inspired generations, and this latest version honors that heritage while adding modern technology. The result is a car that looks stunning and drives even better.
