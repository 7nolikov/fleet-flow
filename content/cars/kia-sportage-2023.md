The Kia Sportage has evolved from a value-focused SUV into a design icon. Its boomerang-shaped LED lights and dramatic curves make it one of the most distinctive vehicles in its class.

## Diesel Efficiency

For those covering serious miles, the 1.6-litre diesel engine offers exceptional fuel economy—expect 5.5L/100km on motorways—while delivering enough torque for confident overtaking.

## Curved Display Innovation

The panoramic curved display seamlessly integrates the digital instruments with the infotainment screen, creating a driver-focused cockpit that looks and feels futuristic.

## Perfect For

- **Long-distance travelers**: Diesel efficiency for serious mileage
- **Design lovers**: Head-turning style at every stop
- **Families**: Spacious, practical, and packed with safety features
- **Value seekers**: Premium features at mainstream prices
